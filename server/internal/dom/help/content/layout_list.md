## List layout

The list layout is the one-task-per-row reading view. Rows are dense and ordered by your personal sort (drag to reorder), so it works well as an inbox or a daily-focus screen.

**Best for:** the working set of tasks one person is responsible for, in the order they intend to handle them.

**Affordances:**
- Drag the row handle to reorder; the new position is saved per user.
- The transition bar at the right of each row shows the next legal flow steps.
- Quick-entry at the bottom adds a new task in-place without leaving the view.
