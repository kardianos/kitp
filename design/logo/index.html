Concept: two inputs, one reactor draining them. Steel inputs (the raw work) are consumed by the confident UniFi-blue reactor. Each mark is built on a 48-unit grid and shown below at real favicon sizes (16–32 px) on light and dark, plus a 96 px hero.

1. Converge

Two streams fall together into a core that drips out the base — a literal drain.

2. Intake

A solid reactor disc with an open core; two side feeds are pulled into its shoulders.

3. Funnel

Two task cards tip into a funnel that narrows to a single draining drop. Most literal.

4. Reactor

A hexagonal core with an open drain; two feeds enter the top corners. Most "engine".
